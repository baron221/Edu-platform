# Welcome to EduNationUz 🎓

Welcome to **EduNationUz**, your premium online learning platform! This guide will help you navigate the platform, discover new skills, and get the most out of your learning experience.

---

## 🌟 Getting Started

### Creating an Account
Joining EduNationUz is simple and free:
1. Click **Sign Up** on the homepage.
2. You can register using your **Email**, **Phone Number**, or quickly connect using **Google**, **GitHub**, or **Telegram**.
3. Once registered, you will receive a welcome email, and you're ready to start learning!

### Logging In & Password Recovery
- If you already have an account, click **Log In** and enter your credentials.
- **Forgot your password?** Don't worry! Click the *"Forgot password?"* link on the login page, enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset it.

---

## 📚 Exploring & Taking Courses

EduNationUz offers a wide variety of courses, from beginner tutorials to advanced masterclasses.

### Finding a Course
- Browse the **Courses** page to see all available content.
- Courses are labeled as either **Free** or **Premium**.
- Click on any course to view its curriculum, instructor details, and a preview video.

### Enrolling in a Course
- **Free Courses:** Simply click "Start Learning" to jump right in!
- **Premium Courses:** Click "Enroll Now" to purchase the course. You can pay securely using **Payme**, **Click**, or **Stripe**. Once payment is confirmed, the course is permanently unlocked in your account.

### The Learning Experience
Our dynamic course viewer is designed for focus and progress:
- **Video Player:** High-quality, adaptive video streaming.
- **Progress Tracking:** As you finish watching a video, the lesson will automatically be marked as complete, and your overall course progress bar will update.
- **Course Content:** Easily jump between chapters and lessons using the sidebar.

---

## 🤖 The AI Tutor

Every course comes with an intelligent AI teaching assistant powered by Google Gemini.
- **Ask Questions:** Unsure about a concept in the video? Open the AI Tutor chat and ask! It understands the context of the course you are currently watching.
- **Upload Files/Images:** You can upload screenshots of your code or assignments, and the AI will help you debug or explain the material.
- **Voice Typing:** Don't want to type? Use the microphone to speak your question directly to the AI Tutor.

---

## 📅 Expert Sessions & 1-on-1 Mentorship

Sometimes you just need to talk to a human expert. EduNationUz allows you to book private, 1-on-1 video sessions with industry professionals.

1. Navigate to the **Experts** directory.
2. Find an instructor whose skills match what you want to learn.
3. Select an available time slot and book your session.
4. You will automatically receive a **Google Meet link** via email and Telegram right before your session begins!

---

## ⚙️ Managing Your Account

- **Dashboard:** Access your personal dashboard to see your enrolled courses, overall progress, and upcoming expert sessions.
- **Certificates:** Upon completing 100% of a course, you will receive a verifiable Certificate of Completion that you can download as a PDF or share on LinkedIn.
- **Settings:** Update your profile picture, change your display name, or update your notification preferences in the Account Settings.
